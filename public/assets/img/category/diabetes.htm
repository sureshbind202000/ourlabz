Diabetes is one of the most common ailments that affect both men and women. The disease occurs when your blood glucose levels are too high. If the body cannot produce insulin, a hormone that converts glucose into energy, glucose remains in your blood, preventing it from reaching your cells. An inadequate supply of blood glucose can cause further complications. Abnormal levels of glucose in your blood can result in type 1 and type 2 diabetes.

Type 1 diabetes does not allow your body to produce insulin, and this disease can affect adults and children at any age. On the other hand, type 2 diabetes restricts your body from producing or using insulin appropriately. It is possible to suffer from type 2 diabetes symptoms from a young age, and the condition can increase the likelihood of multiple health issues, including:

  • Cardiovascular diseases 
  • Kidney damage
  • Skin infections and problems 
  • Alzheimer's disease 
  • Hearing problems or loss 
  • Nerve damage 
  • Dental issues 
  • Foot complications 
  • Vision problems

To address these symptoms properly, you will need medical attention. Apollo 24|7 provides high-quality diabetes test packages for your convenience.

Risk Factors of Diabetes

You might experience diabetes symptoms if you have a family history of the disease. Other risk factors for diabetes include your race and ethnicity, which makes you prone to the disease.
